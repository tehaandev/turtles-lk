Kosgoda Turtle Hatchery

Kosgoda turtle hatchery is a non-profit organization located on the southern coast of Sri Lanka. The hatchery plays a vital role in the conservation of sea turtles by rescuing and incubating turtle eggs and releasing the hatchlings into the ocean. Visitors can take a guided tour to learn about the different turtle species and their habitats, as well as witness the hatchery's conservation efforts. The hatchery also conducts outreach programs with local communities to raise awareness about the importance of protecting sea turtles and their habitats. As one of the oldest turtle hatcheries in Sri Lanka, Kosgoda has been instrumental in protecting the turtle population in the region and preserving the fragile ecosystem of the coastal area.

About Us

Sea turtle safety and conservation are our top priorities at our turtle hatchery. We remove turtle eggs from unsafe nesting locations and carefully incubate them until they are mature enough to be returned to the ocean. Our goal is to teach people about sea turtle protection while also providing guided tours of our hatchery. We are proud of our dedication to environmentally friendly techniques and responsible tourism, and we work together with regional communities to further these practices. Come to our hatchery to help us safeguard these incredible creatures.
